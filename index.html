What Makes Our Lead Generation Different

Find and Drive is consumer-focused at its core. Before a single rand is spent on advertising, we invest time researching, understanding, and collecting data on South African car buying audiences. Most campaigns in this industry are product-first — whether that product is the car or the finance deal. Ours treat the buyer as the starting point.

The result: campaigns built around real buyer profiles, not assumed ones. Accuracy comes first — understanding exactly who is in the market and why. Efficiency follows. That sequence is what makes paid advertising genuinely effective rather than just expensive.

Stage 01 · Problem Definition
Understand the Stock & the Challenge
We start with the vehicle and the commercial reality. Example: Ford Puma residual values were inaccurate — banks needed to move these units. That becomes the brief. Who needs to buy this car, and why?
Stage 02 · Market Discovery
Consumer Sentiment & Demand Signals
We analyse online search volumes, consumer sentiment, and the vehicle's value proposition to identify which audiences are naturally aligned. Demand data drives decisions — not assumptions.
Stage 03 · Audience Profiling
Map the Car to the Right Buyer
Once the audience is identified, we profile them in sufficient depth — demographics, behaviour, financial profile, intent signals. This profiling is what allows us to build campaigns that resonate and convert.
Stage 04 · Campaign Efficiency
Remove Wastage. Optimise for Intent.
The OEM has already spent millions on awareness. We do not compete with that. We skip broad reach and optimise directly for specific, in-market audiences — targeting people ready to buy, not people ready to browse.
Our Positioning
Audience-First.
Not Product-First.
Other campaigns in the market are product-first — built around a specific car or a finance offering, with VAF client acquisition as the end goal. Find and Drive campaigns are built around who is buying, giving us a structural edge in lead quality and conversion rates.
